Add a customer improvement suggestion feature to CompanyHQ.

---

## WHAT TO BUILD

Customers can submit improvement suggestions from their portal. Admin reviews them and updates their status. Customers get automatic email notifications when their suggestion status changes.

---

## PART 1 — Suggest button in customer portal

Add a small feedback/suggestion icon button at the top center of the customer portal dashboard. Use a simple lightbulb or speech bubble icon that matches the overall app icon style.

Clicking it opens a simple modal with:
- Title: "Suggest an Improvement"
- A text field: "What's your idea?" (required, max 200 characters)
- A larger text area: "Tell us more" (optional, max 1000 characters)
- Submit button
- Cancel button

On submit:
- Save the suggestion
- Show a thank you message: "Thanks! We'll review your idea and keep you posted."
- Send an automatic email to the customer confirming receipt using SendGrid
- Send an internal notification to Admin that a new suggestion was submitted

---

## PART 2 — Customer suggestion status page

Add a "My Suggestions" section to the customer portal (can be a tab or small section on their dashboard) where they can see:
- A list of their own suggestions only
- Each shows: title, date submitted, current status badge
- Status options: Received | Reviewing | Planned | Completed | Not Planned
- Clicking a suggestion shows the full details including any status notes Admin added

Customers cannot see other customers' suggestions.

---

## PART 3 — Admin review in Admin Panel

Add a "Customer Suggestions" section to the Admin Panel showing all submitted suggestions. Each suggestion shows:
- Customer name
- Suggestion title and description
- Date submitted
- Current status
- A dropdown to change the status
- A "Status Note" field where Admin can add a short note explaining the decision (optional)
- This note is visible to the customer

When Admin changes the status, automatically send an email to the customer notifying them, including the new status and any note Admin added.

---

## PART 4 — Email templates

Use SendGrid for all emails. FROM: dan@chapinlandscapes.com

Email 1 — Confirmation (sent to customer on submission):
Subject: "We received your idea!"
Body: Thank them, tell them they'll hear back when there's an update, include their suggestion title.

Email 2 — Status update (sent to customer when Admin changes status):
Subject: "Update on your suggestion: [suggestion title]"
Body: Tell them the new status, include any note from Admin, thank them for the feedback.

---

## DATABASE

Create a `customer_suggestions` table:
- id
- customer_id (foreign key)
- title
- description
- status (enum: received, reviewing, planned, completed, not_planned) default: received
- admin_note (text, optional)
- created_at
- updated_at

---

## NOTES
- Customers only ever see their own suggestions
- Do not add this feature to any non-customer role view except the Admin Panel review section
- Use existing styling patterns
- Do not break any existing customer portal functionality
