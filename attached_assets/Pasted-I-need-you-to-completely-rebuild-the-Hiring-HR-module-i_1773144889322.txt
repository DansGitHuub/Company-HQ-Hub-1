SECTION 2: AUTOMATED NOTIFICATIONS
Every time a card moves to a new stage, the system automatically sends communications. Build this trigger system:
New Application received:

Email to applicant: "We received your application for [Position] at Chapin Landscapes. We'll be in touch within 2–3 business days."
In-app notification to HR/Manager: "New application received — [Name] for [Position]"

Moved to Review:

In-app notification to HR: "[Name] moved to Review"

Moved to Phone Screen:

Email to applicant: "We'd like to schedule a quick phone call. Please reply with your availability or call us at [phone]."
In-app notification to interviewer assigned

Moved to Interview:

Email to applicant: "You're invited to interview for [Position]. Your interview is scheduled for [date/time] at [location]."
SMS to applicant: "Reminder: Your interview at Chapin Landscapes is [date] at [time]. Reply CONFIRM to confirm."

Moved to Offer Extended:

Email to applicant: "We'd like to extend an offer for the [Position] role. Please review the attached offer letter and respond within 48 hours."
In-app notification to manager

Moved to Hired:

Email to applicant: "Welcome to Chapin Landscapes! We're excited to have you join the team. Please complete the onboarding documents attached."
SMS to applicant: "Welcome to Chapin Landscapes! Check your email for next steps."
Automatically create Employee Record (see Section 3)
Automatically trigger onboarding checklist
In-app notification to all managers: "New hire — [Name] starts [start date]"

Moved to Not a Fit:

Email to applicant: "Thank you for your interest in Chapin Landscapes. After careful consideration, we've decided to move forward with other candidates. We appreciate your time."

All emails should be editable templates that admins can customize in Admin Panel settings. For now, use the above as the default text. Use the existing email/notification infrastructure in the project. If no email system exists yet, use nodemailer with environment variables for SMTP config.
